I'm a final-year PhD student at Queen Mary University of London, supervised by Haim Dubossarsky. I work on how language models represent meaning — and on what those representations capture, distort, and miss.

My research runs across mechanistic interpretability, representation modelling, lexical semantic change detection, cross-lingual transfer for low-resource languages, and information retrieval. The thread tying them together is a single question: how is meaning encoded inside a model, and how does it move — across senses, across time, and across languages?
